Casino with Uppaal

Artifacts
  • environment model producing method calls
  • contract model reacting to calls (automaton with single central place)
Investigated Properties
  • safety property
  • lifeness possible, but not modelled
  • timed properties possible, but not yet modelled
Verification Techniques
  • CTL model checking
Can offer:
fully-automated verification of small instances (e.g. only two players) with tightly bounded variable domains (e.g. maximum pot 8)
Looking for:
  • a generalization of the proofs for larger variable domains and arbitrary numbers of components
  • abstractions that can be used by the model checker / symbolic representations of the state space
Author(s)
Jonas Becker, Paula Herber
